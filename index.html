FAQ QUESTIONS

We tried to answer all questions asked by our guests.

Is there video surveillance in your halls? None of our rooms are equipped with a video monitoring system. And our managers always welcome all our guests as if it was their first time. We perfectly understand how important this issue is, which is why we pay special attention to it.

Can I really take any food with me? You can bring your own food and drinks or order them from any restaurant/cafe near the cinema. We offer fondue, snacks, popcorn, drinks and ice directly from us. We organize table setting and provide all necessary utensils (plates, glasses, cutlery, etc.).

How often are the rooms cleaned? We clean up after each guest. Before previous guests leave, we check whether everything is OK with the appliances and furniture. Please be advised that the booked hours include 10 minutes for our employee to clean the room.
